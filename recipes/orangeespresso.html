Orange Espresso

Home

Orange espresso, or "Spr-OJ," is a refreshing, citrusy coffee beverage made by combining a double shot of espresso with of cold orange juice over ice. Often featuring a citrus-forward, fruity flavor profile, it is popular in Thailand, sometimes enhanced with tonic water or simple syrup for added sweetness and fizz. Below are the ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Tube Ice
  • Orange
  • Sweetener

Step-by-Step

  1. Pull a double shot espresso.
  2. In your glass, add ice and pour in orange juice
  3. Add espresso.
