Spanish Latte

Home

This is an iced spanish latte you can make at home. Please see recipe below:

Ingredients

  • tube ice
  • condensed milk
  • espresso, 1 shot
  • milk

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Extract one shot of espresso.
  2. Add condensed milk at the bottom of the cup.
  3. Add espresso on the cup and stir thoroughly.
  4. Add steamed milk.
